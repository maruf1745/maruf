Why Coca Leaves?

The coca plant is native to the Andes region of South America and began being used in many
natural forms of treatment. Many of its cultivators would chew the leaf of the plant for stimulation and
endurance. Today, the coca leaf is most commonly found in countries like Peru and Bolivia, and used in the
form of teas to alleviate common symptoms such as altitude sickness, gastrointestinal issues, joint pain
and fatigue, only to name a few. We are proud tointroduce Bliss Coca Water, made with a hint of pure coca
leaves containing essential antioxidants, vitamins, and properties to help boost your energy!
